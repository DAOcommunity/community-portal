    Grant Applications Submitted

    We fund meetups all over the world to support education around Ethereum, DeFi, and MakerDAO.
    Over 40,000 Dai has been granted to over 100 meetups serving over 5,600 people across 33 countries since February 2019.

    What We Do Fund

    • Honorarium for organizers and speakers
    • Space rental
    • Equipment rental
    • Food and drinks

    What We Don't Fund

    • Burner wallet speakeasy events
    • Bar tabs or alcoholic beverages
    • Dai or xDai airdrops
    • Banners
    • T-shirts
    • Paid marketing

    Get Funded

    Looking to fund a larger event? Please reach out to events@makerdao.com.
    Whether this is your first meetup or you're an experienced organizer, please follow these steps to apply for funding.
    After submitting your application form, please allow three to five business days for a response.

    Fill in the Application Form

    This form gives us all the information we need to make a decision on your funding request.

    Once Your Meetup is Funded...

    Promote Community Development

    If we fund your meetup we’ll ask you to speak briefly about Community Development at MakerDAO.
    • Direct people here to this site
    • Mention that there are lots of ways to contribute to MakerDAO
    • Mention that we award Dai bounties varying by the type of contribution.

    Take Pictures and Video

    Recording the talks and hosting them on online can be a very effective way of providing resources for others in your local community to share between each other and even with friends of neighbouring cities and countries.
    Media provides very useful learning resources, gives you something you can send to people remotely, and generally gives you a larger reach.
    4

    After the Event, Fill Out the Feedback Form

    This form helps us track metrics and collect media and resources that are produced as a result of these events.
    Feedback form ↗
    After your event you will recieve a follow up email requesting the completion of a feedback form.
    Grantees who do not fill out this form will not be eligible for future funding.
