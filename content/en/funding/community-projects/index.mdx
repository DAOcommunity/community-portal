# Community Projects

## Apply for up to 5000 Dai to help improve the Maker community

Propose your own projects or see our [help wanted board](https://github.com/makerdao/community/projects/2?card_filter_query=label%3A%22help+wanted%22) and comment on open issues or contact the owner.

### Community Development Initiative Proposals (CDIPs)

We use a process involving Community Development Initiative Proposals (CDIPs) to evaluate funding for self-directed projects.

A CDIP is the scoping and framing tool for anyone interested in receiving funds for proposing new MakerDAO community initiatives or resources.
